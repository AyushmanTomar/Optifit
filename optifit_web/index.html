About OPTIFIT

  • OPTIFIT offers an extensive database with accurate calorie counts for a wide range of sports and activities. Whether it's running, swimming, cycling, or even martial arts, users can easily track their calorie burn and progress
  • OPTIFIT targets individuals who are interested in improving their overall fitness and wellbeing. The app caters to different customer segments, including fitness enthusiasts, beginners looking to adopt a healthier lifestyle, and people with specific fitness goals such as weight loss, muscle gain, or improved cardiovascular health.
